Germany

Germany, officially the Federal Republic of Germany, is a country in Central Europe. It is the second most populous country in Europe after Russia, and the most populous member state of the European Union. Germany is situated between the Baltic and North seas to the north, and the Alps to the south; it covers an area of 357,022 square kilometers, with a population of over 83 million within its 16 constituent states. Its capital and largest city is Berlin, and its financial center is Frankfurt; the largest urban area is the Ruhr.
